Description

An easy lasagna recipe that will save you lots of time, and keeps the kids happy. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

This lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredients list

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32oz of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
